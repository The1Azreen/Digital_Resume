Vegetable Shooter is an object-oriented shoot 'em up (shmup) game where the player controls a spaceship to shoot down incoming waves of vegetable enemies! The game is built using Java, and developed in Eclipse IDE. In Vegetable Shooter, you must defend your spaceship by shooting down waves of vegetables (carrots, tomatoes, cabbages, etc.) flying towards you. The goal is to eliminate all the vegetables to win each level while avoiding getting hit. It's a fun and quirky take on the traditional shoot 'em up genre!

  • Player controls a spaceship that can shoot projectiles.
  • Waves of vegetable enemies with different behaviors.
  • Score tracking and game over conditions.
  • Increasing difficulty as levels progress.
  • Simple yet addictive gameplay.
